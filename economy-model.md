# MMORPG Economy Model

Defines the virtual economy.

---

## Currency

Primary currency: gold

Possible secondary resources:

- crafting materials
- rare drops

---

## Item Sources

Items enter the economy through:

- monster drops
- crafting
- npc vendors
- dungeon rewards

---

## Item Sinks

Items leave the economy through:

- durability loss
- crafting consumption
- npc buyback removal

---

## Market Simulation

NPC merchants adjust prices based on:

supply
demand

Example:

low supply → higher price

high supply → lower price

---

## Player Trading

Players can trade:

- items
- currency

Possible trading systems:

- direct trade
- auction house
- market stalls

---

## Long Term Goal

Self-balancing economy with minimal inflation.
