# MMORPG System Map

Overview of all major gameplay systems.

---

## Core Systems

PlayerSystem

Handles:

- player login
- player state
- leveling
- stats

---

CombatSystem

Handles:

- damage calculation
- cooldowns
- aggro

---

InventorySystem

Handles:

- items
- equipment
- stacking

---

EconomySystem

Handles:

- npc vendors
- player trading
- market simulation

---

GuildSystem

Handles:

- guild creation
- guild chat
- alliances

---

QuestSystem

Handles:

- quest tracking
- quest rewards
- progression

---

WorldSystem

Handles:

- terrain
- chunk loading
- world persistence

---

NPCSystem

Handles:

- npc spawning
- ai behaviour
- pathfinding
